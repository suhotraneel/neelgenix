Project Overview

Right to Look is an immersive audio-visual art gallery experience. By scanning a QR code, visitors access a web app that guides them through the exhibition. Curated audio tours accompany each artwork, allowing users to explore the museum, discover the stories behind each piece, and hear directly the curators' voices.

Target Group

Individuals, aged 18-45, visiting or present in Mumbai, including students, employed professionals, workers in unorganized sectors and entrepreneurs with a strong presence in the city's cultural and social scene, with a casual interest in art exhibitions.

Problem Statement

"Art gallery visitors often lack sufficient contextual information and engaging experiences to fully appreciate artworks and understand the artists' intentions. This results in a diminished connection with the art, and therefore, reduced lasting engagement and visitor retention for the gallery."

Phase 3: Develop

Solution Concept

An immersive and accessible web application, with a seamless QR code onboarding process, that delivers curated audio-visual experiences for art exhibitions, providing deeper contextual information about artworks and artists to enhance visitor engagement and increase art appreciation.
